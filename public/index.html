Clean Peaks Laboratories is committed to providing the medical and recreational cannabis community with fast, reliable quantification of their samples. Currently, we offer three services: Primary Cannabinoid (d9-THC, CBD, and CBN) Quantification, Primary Terpene (alpha & beta pinene, limonene, linalool, and beta-caryophyllene) Quantification, and Residual Solvent Quantification. We are new in the cannabis testing space. We are excited to be a part of the cannabis testing community, and as we scale up we will provide more services.
